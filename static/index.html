Welcome to EIDO Sentinel

EIDO Sentinel is an in-development AI agent engineered to dramatically improve how emergency communications and incident reports are managed. With leveraging Large Language Models and modern data processing, EIDO Sentinel offers efficient, intelligent, and responsive framework for public safety operations.

We tackle the challenge of diverse data formats, information overload, and the need for rapid sense-making in critical situations; hoping to organize structured NENA EIDO JSON messages or unstructured raw text alerts, EIDO Sentinel transforms raw data into actionable intelligence.

The Power of Agentic AI Workflows

Agentic AI moves beyond simple processing to systems that can plan, reason, and use tools autonomously. For EIDO Sentinel, this could mean:

  • Proactive Monitoring: Continuously monitoring data streams to flag potential incidents.
  • Autonomous Tool Use: Querying GIS, weather APIs, or internal databases automatically.
  • Complex Decision Support: Assisting in multi-step decision-making by combining LLM reasoning with structured data and tool outputs.

Precision & Privacy: Fine-tuning and Local LLMs

For mission-critical public safety applications, accuracy, reliability, and privacy are non-negotiable. EIDO Sentinel's architecture is designed with these in mind.
